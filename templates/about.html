About Harare Mobile Mechanics

Redefining automotive repair services in Zimbabwe

Our Mission

At Harare Mobile Mechanics, our mission is to transform the automotive repair experience by bringing skilled mechanics directly to our customers, saving them time and eliminating the inconvenience of traditional repair shops.

We're committed to providing transparent, high-quality service at competitive prices, while empowering local mechanics to grow their businesses through our platform.

Our Vision

We envision a future where vehicle maintenance is no longer a disruption to your day. Our goal is to build Zimbabwe's largest network of mobile mechanics, making professional automotive services accessible to everyone, everywhere in Harare and beyond.

Our Journey

2018

The Beginning

Harare Mobile Mechanics started as a one-man operation, with our founder Takunda providing mobile repairs to friends and family around Harare.

2020

Growing the Network

We expanded our network to include 10 certified mechanics serving the greater Harare area, introducing a phone-based booking system.

2021

Digital Transformation

Launched our website and booking platform to streamline the process for customers and mechanics, significantly improving efficiency.

Today

Leading the Industry

Now with over 50 skilled mechanics in our network, we're Zimbabwe's premier mobile mechanic service, with plans to expand to more cities.
